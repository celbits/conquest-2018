Hello There

What We Do

Conquest is an International Startup Conclave organized by the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership, BITS Pilani. Conquest provides an exemplary platform for young, fresh startups to come to the fore and grow. We provide a six-week long summer mentorship program with weekly mentoring sessions for our Top 10 startups in Bangalore followed by the Grand Finale at the end of July. We provide free accommodation, workspace and intensive mentorship to the startups during this period thus building India’s first student run startup launchpad.

Why Conquest?

We’ve got everything you need to launch and grow your business

Startup Launchpad

Our top 10 startups get free accomodation and office space where they will be mentored by top VCs and startup founders and learn how to run their business better and raise funds.

Mentoring Sessions

We hold mentoring sessions for top 50 starups in Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore where startups get valuable insights from India's top investors and founders.

Media Outreach

Conquest gets featured in top media houses of the country and is a great platform to showcase the true potential of the startup to the entire world.

Conquest Community

Conquest Community plans to bring founders, investor and mentors under one roof, creating a conducive environment for learning and networking.

Raising Funds

Investors from top VC firms, Conquest and experienced founders help the startup craft their pitch, build their deck, maneuver deals and secure fundings.

Valuable Resources

We provide resources worth 1 million USD, which help startups attain exponential growth and makes them more efficient.
