Hello There

What We Do

Conquest, an initiative by the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership, BITS Pilani, is India’s first Student-Run Startup Launchpad. We help the best founders build great businesses and raise money. We provide a six-week long summer mentorship program at Bangalore to ten early age startups from all across the country. We give them a great place to live and work together, advice from people who have built great companies themselves and the people who are leaders in their fields. The program ends with the Grand Finale where the ten startups get to pitch before India’s biggest investment firms and media houses.

Why register for conquest?

Our Menotors

Top 10 startups get mentored by industry stalwarts through a 6 week mentorship program in Bangalore. By providing free accommodation and co-working space, we create a co-learning environment where founders together learn how to run their business and raise funds.

Valuable Resources

Conquest provides resources worth 1 million USD which play a pivotal role in helping startups scale up and run operations. These resources are handpicked from a pool of resources and thus ensure maximum growth for startups.

Media Outreach

Conquest gets featured in the top media firms of the country and thus serves as a perfect platform for startups to gain exposure and showcase themselves to the world.

Conquest Community

Conquest Community plans to bring together founders, investors and mentors of all editions of Conquest under one roof, creating a conducive environment for learning and networking.

Raising Funds

Conquest with its panel of experienced founders and investors from top VC firms help the startups craft their pitch, build their deck, maneuver deals and secure funding.

Mentoring Sessions

We hold mentoring sessions for top 50 startups in Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore where startups get valuable insights from India’s top investors and founders.

How It Works

Our Model

Registration

Registrations for Conquest will open from 25th February and will go on till 15th April. Startups who are past proof-of-concept can register by filling in a simple application form. There is no fee to register for Conquest. The shortlisting will take place on parameters like idea, team, progress, understanding of market and competition.




Mentorship Program

The top 10 startups move into Bangalore for the 6 week long mentorship program where they are provided with free accommodation and working space. Conquest aims at creating an environment of co-learning which helps early stage startups grow together at exponential pace. The program will comprise of weekly mentorship lunches, networking events and a wide array of activities that will ensure that startups are ready for fundraising at the end of the 6 weeks.



Mentoring Sessions

The top 50 startups get to be mentored one on one by stalwarts from the entrepreneurial world. These mentoring sessions are held in Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore. Startups can choose one of these cities. Mentors from different verticals like Customer Acquisition, Product Management, Fundraising etc come in for a day and guide startups through the path in their respective verticals.
