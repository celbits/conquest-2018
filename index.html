What We Do

Conquest is an International Startup Conclave organized by the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership, BITS Pilani. Conquest provides an exemplary platform for young, fresh startups to come to the fore and grow. We provide a six-week long summer mentorship program with weekly mentoring sessions for our Top 10 startups in Bangalore followed by the Grand Finale at the end of July. We provide free accommodation, workspace and intensive mentorship to the startups during this period thus building India’s first student run startup launchpad.

Conclave Format

An exciting mentoring session culminating in the Grand Finale

Round 1

Registrations for Conquest 2018 will be launched on the 15th of March. The application process will be conducted online where in startups will fill in information about their idea, business model, team and scale of operations.

Round 2

The goal of the first round of screening is to find teams who have the foresight and ambition to work towards big problems.

The goal of the first round of screening is to find teams who have the foresight and ambition to work towards big problems.

Round 3

Three Mentoring Sessions will be conducted for the top 50 startups in Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore, giving them an opportunity to interact personally with ecosystem stalwarts and freely clarify any doubts that they have. The startups will be provided with mentorship in various categories, including Fundraising, Product Management, Customer Acquisition, Tech, and Scaling up & Operations.

Round 5

The Top 10 startups will move in Bangalore. They will stay and work together for six weeks along with intensive mentoring with weekly mentoring lunches with successful founders and online mentoring for help in Customer Acquisition, Tech, Legal and other verticals.

Round 6

The Grand Finale of Conquest 2018 will feature our Top 10 startups pitching in front of prominent investors and stalwarts in the startup community. The Grand Finale of Conquest 2017 was a star-studded affair held in, Hyatt Regency, New Delhi. The event commenced with a speech by Mrs Bindu Dey, Secretary, Technology Development Board and was followed by open door startup pitches, panel discussions, a fishbowl session and the keynote speech by Rishabh Kaul, Co-Founder, Belong.
